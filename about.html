Mariia Minieieva is a digital 3D artist based in Iowa, USA, whose passion lies in exploring abstract forms through VEX code and mathematical expressions. By studying her two passions, Studio Art and Computer Science, at Grinnell College, she has positioned her art at the intersection of art and technology.

Mariia's primary tools for crafting 3D art include Procreate, Houdini, and After Effects, complemented by Mantra and Redshift rendering engines to add depth and intrigue to her creations.

Before immersing herself in the realm of 3D art, Mariia pursued background in a digital 2D art for 7 years.
